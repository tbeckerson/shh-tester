Socialist HipHop is coming to your town!

On June 1st, SHH will be at the Ligma Ball Field between 8:30 and 8:35 to perform a medley of hits during the halftime break while the players polish their sticks.

June 2nd will be the fundraiser to help with the legal fees incurred by our ally Dee Snutz, during the Bring Our Friends Amnesty event last winter.
